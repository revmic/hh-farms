About Us

We're a startup hopyard in Edwardsville Illinois with 360 plants living on about a quarter acre. We went from discussing our farming possibilities over breakfast on a Sunday morning to having all the infrastructure in place and every plant in the ground within the week, and this is on top of our pesky full time jobs). You can read more about our hopyard setup over at our posts.

Feel free to contact us with any questions or comments. We'd love to hear from you!
